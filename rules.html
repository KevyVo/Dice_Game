Rules

  • - The game has 2 players, playing in rounds
  • - In each turn, a player rolls a dice as many times as they wish. Each roll`s result get added to their ROUND score
  • - If the player rolls a double 1`s, all their ROUND score gets added to their TOTAL score. After that, it's the next player's turn
  • - If the player rolls double 3`s, their ROUND score will be reset and it`s the next player`s turn
  • - If the player tolls a double 6`s their ROUND and TOTAL score will be reset to zero.
  • - The player can choose to 'Hold', which means that his ROUND score gets added to his TOTAL score score. After that, it's the next player's turn
  • - You change the FINAL SCORE by entering a new value into the textbox or you can leave it empty, which default it to 100 points

First player to the FINAL SCORE wins the dice game, have fun!
