Rules

- The game has 2 players, playing in rounds - In each turn, a player rolls a dice as many times as he whishes. Each roll`s result get added to his ROUND score - If the player rolls a 1, all their ROUND score gets added to their TOTAL score. After that, it's the next player's turn - If the player rolls double 3`s, their ROUND score will be reset and it`s the next player`s turn - If the player tolls a double 6`s their ROUND and TOTAL will be reset to zero. - The player can choose to 'Hold', which means that his ROUND score gets added to his GLOBAL score. After that, it's the next player's turn - The first player to reach 100 points on GLOBAL score wins the game
